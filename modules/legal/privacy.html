Cookies

How we use cookies

Cookies are very small text files that are stored on your computer when you visit some websites. Cookies are sent to your browser from a website and stored on your computer's hard drive. REVOLT Wind may use cookies to track shopping basket contents and remember where you are in the order process. You can instruct your computer to disable cookies or indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, if you do not accept cookies, you may stop our website from functioning properly for you.

This Website May:

  • Remember what is in your shopping cart;
  • Remember where you are in the order process;
  • Remember that you are logged in and that your session is secure.

The above features are not strictly necessary but are required to provide you with the best user experience.
