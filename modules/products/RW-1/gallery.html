  • You Determine the Location

    You are not limited to any one location! The RW-1 is lightweight (less than 20 lbs) and portable. It can be easily carried to any windy location.

  • Easy to Build

    Just turn a few screws and you're ready to hang it! It will start making power as soon as the wind blows.

  • Strong

    Made from sturdy stainless steel and aluminum parts, the RW-1 will not rust or corode. The fiberglass reinforced plastic composite blades are UV and impact resistant and will not crack or degrade.

  • Versatile

    The electric power being generated by the RW-1 can be converted to run or charge your device using an appropriate voltage converter.

  • Inexpensive

    The entire system costs about 1/10 less than a tower-mounted windmill of the same size.
